Design challenge

3-minute presentation template

Use this page to prepare your short consulting presentation. You do not need to fill in every field. Focus on clear reasoning and a practical recommendation.

1

Slide 1 — The problem

Explain the case and why this is a trust or coordination problem.

The problem

What to say

  • Start with the real-world problem, not with blockchain.
  • Mention the organizations involved.
  • Explain why a shared and reliable history may be needed.
2

Slide 2 — Our analysis

Decide whether blockchain is useful and what information should go where.

Our analysis

What to say

  • Do not say “blockchain is good” in general — explain why it fits or does not fit this case.
  • Remember: not all data should be stored on-chain.
  • If a normal database is better, that is a valid recommendation.
3

Slide 3 — Final recommendation

Give a clear recommendation to the client.

Final recommendation

Tip: Your final answer can be “No”. A good consultant does not always recommend blockchain. The goal is to make a practical and well-reasoned decision.
